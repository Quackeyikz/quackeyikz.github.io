A Place Where Everything Started

Mondstadt, City of Wind and Freedom

Where the gentle breeze carries dandelion seeds and the sound of freedom rings eternal, there stands Mondstadt - a city blessed by the Anemo Archon himself. Built upon the ideals of freedom and hope, this pastoral paradise welcomes all who seek refuge from tyranny and oppression.

Once ruled by the aristocracy of old, Mondstadt was liberated by the power of wind and song. Now, under the protection of the Knights of Favonius and watched over by the winds of Barbatos, the city flourishes as a beacon of liberty in Teyvat.

From the towering spires of the Cathedral to the bustling markets near the city gates, every corner of Mondstadt tells a story of resilience, courage, and the unbreakable spirit of its people. Here, where the wind never stops blowing, adventure awaits around every corner.
